# Account Deletion Request

**BeachMaster Pro**

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, you may do so directly within the app:
* Go to **Your Profile (top right)** > **Remove Account**

If you no longer have access to the app, you may request deletion via email.

**To request deletion by email:**
1. Send an email to: **[contact@beachmasterpro.space]**
2. Subject Line: **Account Deletion Request**
3. Please include your registered email address in the body of the message.

We will process your request and permanently delete your account and data within 30 days.
